Acme Corporation is a technology company headquartered in San Francisco. It is led by CEO John Smith, who reports to the Board of Directors. Acme operates a Cloud Division and a Legacy Systems Department. The Cloud Division maintains partnerships with AWS and Google Cloud. The Engineering Team manages the product infrastructure. The Legal Department oversees compliance and regulatory matters. Acme's main product is the DataSync Platform, which is used by enterprise clients across the United States and Europe.
